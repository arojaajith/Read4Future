Who we are

The Read4Future is a project designed to help Sri Lankan future generation to be successful in the global economy by helping them prepare for the right opportunities, and empowering them to chart their course to success.

Our Inspiration comes from English for Speakers of Other Languages (ESOL) program conducted in the public schools in the United States, that enables non-English speaking children to speak, read and write in English proficiently in a very short time span.

The project Rad4Future is a brainchild of a Sri Lankan born IT professional in the United States.

waht we do?

Our mission is to cultivate the habit of learning by reading in English language among Sri Lankan youth by providing them with the facility, resources and training and thereby empowering them to be successful in the global economy.

Our strategy includes the following components:

  • -  Cultivating the Habit of Reading
  • -  Increasing proficiency in English language
  • -  Access to the learning and development resources
